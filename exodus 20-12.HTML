Devotional Poems

Scripture:Exodus 20:12

"Honour thy father and thy mother: that thy days may be long upon the land which the lord thy God giveth thee."

Honor and Live Long "Honor thy father and mother," He calls, A commandment that forever stands tall. That thy days may be blessed and long, In love and respect, we grow strong. In wisdom and grace, their counsel we seek, Through life's many roads, both smooth and weak. For in honoring them, we honor God's way, And His promises guide us each day. A family rooted in love and care, Reflects His heart, a bond so rare. So honor and cherish, as God has decreed, For in His will, we find all we need.

Bobby Hueston: "Elevate Your Soul, Embrace the Son"
